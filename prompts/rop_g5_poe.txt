**Step 5: Optimized & Tailored Key Skills Generation**

    c.  **Constraint:** Format as a single, comma-separated string, in **Title Case**, and **ordered by priority**.

**Step 6: Optimized & Tailored Cover Letter Generation**
    a.  **CRITICAL FORMATTING INSTRUCTION:** Draft the cover letter **BODY ONLY**. The output for the cover letter must begin with the first word of the first sentence and end with the final punctuation of the last paragraph. It must **ABSOLUTELY NOT** include any salutation (like 'Dear Hiring Manager,'), header, closing (like 'Sincerely,'), or signature.

    c.  Explicitly state the specific job title being applied for early in the letter.
    d.  Dedicate 1-2 central paragraphs to highlighting experiences from `myResume` that prove capability in key JD requirements, prioritizing quantified achievements.
    e.  Directly integrate 1-2 powerful adjectives from `myTestimonials`.
    f.  **Address Experience Nuance:** Consistently use the strategic experience framing defined in Step 4d, ensuring the stated years of experience align with the JD's requirements.
    g.  **Address Seniority Mismatch:** If Michael's experience significantly exceeds the role's needs, subtly address this by expressing a deliberate interest in the role's specific domain or the company's mission.
    h.  Include a clear call to action.

**Step 7: Filename Generation**

    b.  Generate filenames using the templates: `Michael_Martini_Resume_[JobTitle]` and `Michael_Martini_Cover_[JobTitle]`.

**Step 8: Final Output Assembly & Verification**
